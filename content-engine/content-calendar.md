## Content Type Reference

### Hot Tool Drop (3-4 tweets)
Best for: Individual tool promotion, driving clicks to specific AI Bazaar pages
Structure:
1. Hook: "Found a tool this week that [specific benefit]"
2. What it does, who it's for
3. Your honest take (pros AND cons)
4. CTA: Link to AI Bazaar page

### Top 5 Thread (7-8 tweets)
Best for: Tuesday/Wednesday, highest engagement potential
Structure:
1. Hook: "Top 5 AI tools blowing up this week" (thread)
2-6. One tool per tweet (screenshot + 2 lines + link)
7. CTA: "Follow for daily AI tool alpha. Full list: [AI Bazaar link]"

### VS Comparison (3-4 tweets)
Best for: Driving discussion, getting replies
Structure:
1. Hook: "[Tool A] vs [Tool B] — which is actually better?"
2. Quick pros/cons of each
3. Your verdict (be opinionated)
4. CTA: "Full comparison: [AI Bazaar link]"

### Web3 + AI Angle (2-3 tweets)
Best for: Engaging crypto audience specifically
Structure:
1. Hook: "3 AI tools every [crypto role] should be using"
2-3. Tools with Web3-specific use cases
4. CTA: Link to AI Bazaar category page

### Quick Take (1 tweet)
Best for: Engagement, building reputation, weekends
Structure: Hot take on AI news/launch. No link needed. Ask a question or be contrarian.
